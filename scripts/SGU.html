SGU 2019 Science or Fiction Stats

Wayne Heller

December 22, 2019

Background

The Skeptics Guide to the Universe is a weekly podcast hosted by Dr.Steven Novella. Each week during this segment of the podcast, Dr.Novella presents his panel of skeptics with 3 or 4 science-related news items, 1 of which is fiction. And each panelist must determine which is the fiction.

Year End Episode

At the end of the calendar year, Dr.Novella host a year in review episode where the panel reviews their performance at Science or Fiction.

2019 Summary - Episodes# 704 - 754

  • NOTE: Episode 715 excluded - the correct answer was contested and negated in a subsequent episode.
  • Cara Wins Overall and had best performance on questions with and withouts themes
  • Panelist performance on questions without a theme decreased dramatically from last year (except for Cara)
  • The panel performs best with Cara or Bob answer first
  • Going first is a disadvantage for everyone except Cara this year
  • Steve had twice as many host sweeps this year compared to last
  • There were fewer panelist sweeps this year compared to last
  • Steve snapped a 6 game losing streak as panelist starting with episode 686.
  • Bob should consider GWC Go with Cara more often
  • Over the last 2 years, the answering order of {Cara, Jay, Evan, Bob} and {Cara, Bob, Evan, Jay} have answered correctly over 80% of the time.
  • Over the same period, the answering order of {Jay, Bob, Evan, Cara} has only answered correctly 33% of the time.
