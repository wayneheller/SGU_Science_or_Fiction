SGU 2021 Science or Fiction Stats

Wayne Heller

January 2, 2022

Background

The Skeptics Guide to the Universe is a weekly podcast hosted by Dr.Steven Novella. Each week during this segment of the podcast, Dr.Novella presents his panel of skeptics with 3 or 4 science-related news items, 1 of which is fiction. And each panelist must determine which is the fiction.

Year End Episode

At the end of the calendar year, Dr.Novella host a year in review episode where the panel reviews their performance at Science or Fiction.

2021 Summary - Episodes# 808 - 860

  • Overall Winner; Cara (67.4%)

  • Second Place: Jay (62.5%), Third Place: Evan (59.2%), Fourth Place: Bob (51.0%)

  • Overall, the panel guessed correct 59% of the time in 2021 compared to 57% of the time in 2020

  • Most solo Wins: Cara, Jay and Evan (3)

  • Longest Consecutive Win Streak: Evan (8) and Bob (8, spanning end of 2020 into 2021)

  • Longest Consecutive Losing Streak: Jay (4), Note that Georges losing streak is currently 6 starting back at episode 763 at the start of 2020.

  • The panel performed bests when Bob answers first (71%). Bob was last in this category in 2020 and first in 2021.

  • Bob answered correctly 57% of the time when answering first which is exceptionally high compared to the average this year and past years. Overall the panel as a group performed a bit worse when answering first this year compared to last 38% compared to 42%. Steve, please note that Evan was asked to answer first 16 times, Bob 14 times, Cara 11 times and Jay 10 times.

  • Steve swept the panel 5 times this year compared to 7 last year.

  • The panel swept Steve 10 times this year same as last year.

  • Since 2018, the answering order of Bob, Jay, Evan and Cara has answered 82% correctly during that time - 23 correct guesses out of 28 possible and includes the most panelist sweeps (4). In comparison, the answering order of Evan, Bob, Cara, Jay has answered 42% correctly during this time - 15 correct guesses out of 36 possible with no panelist sweeps.

Summary By Rouge:

  • Cara has the highest win percentage overall (67%), the most solo wins (3) tied with Jay and Evan, the highest win percentage on segments with themes (72%).

  • Jay has the second highest win percentage overall (62%) and tied with Cara and Evan for most solo wins (3).

  • Evan has the third highest win percentage overall (59%), the highest win percentage on segments without themes (61%), and is tied with Bob with the longest win streak (8), and is tied with Cara and Jay for most solo wins (3).

  • Bob has the fourth highest win percentage overall (51%) and is tied with Evan with longest win streak (8) - his streak carried over from 2020.
